📜 The Rules of the Conversation

  1. Contribute your own ideas. Add arguments, counter-arguments, and original thoughts inspired by the text.
  2. Engage the material directly. Agree, disagree, or extend the author's claims with your own reasoning.
  3. Build bridges across cultures. Connect ideas to Eastern philosophy, African wisdom traditions, Indian thought, Indigenous knowledge, or any tradition that illuminates the conversation.
  4. Seek the ideal life. This is ultimately a philosophical exercise — we are asking: how should one live?
  5. Be charitable. Assume the best interpretation of others' contributions before critiquing them.
